Wooper Wednesday

It's Wooper Wednesday again!

Every Wednesday is Wooper Wednesday. Come on in and join us celebrate!

Wooper is a beloved Pokémon character that evolves to Quagsire at Level 20. Wooper is an armless axolotol covered in a film of toxic substance and when touched can cause a shooting pain.

Wooper is not only a meme, but also a perfect Pokémon character that has great stats to help you take down your opponent.

Not only is he a good Pokémon character, but he is also adorable! I hope you enjoyed learning about Wooper today and see you next time!
