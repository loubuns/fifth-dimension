Outline of the dimension

This dimension is made up of many parts. The most important of which you are going to visit are:

  • Base
  • Planetarium
  • Lab
  • Vault

The base is your 'home'. This is where your dashboard is to be found.
The planetarium is the 'world map'. Choose your planet and explore it's properties.
The lab is your experiment room. Here you can view your finished and current experiments.
The vault is a very secret place that requires a passcode. Only the worthy get in...
